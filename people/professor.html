Biography

Sung-Yun Park received the B.S. degree in electrical engineering from the Pusan National University, Busan, South Korea in 2005, and the M.S. degree in electrical engineering from the Korean Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), Daejeon, South Korea in 2008, where he was involved in photonics device research. From 2008 to 2010, he worked for Fairchild Semiconductor, Bucheon, South Korea, as a member of technical staff for high voltage mixed signal circuit design. He continued his study in integrated circuits from the Cornell University, Ithaca, NY, USA and received the M. Eng. degree in eectrical and computer engineering in 2011, and the Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering and computer science from the University of Michigan in 2016. From 2016 to 2018, he was a research fellow, and from 2018 he was an assistant research scientist at the University of Michigan. In 2019, he joined in the department of electronics engineering in the Pusan National University as an assistant professor, and from 2024 he is an associate professor.
